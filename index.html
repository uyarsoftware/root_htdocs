Automative Aftermarket Industry

The automotive after market industry is a very active and growing sector.With the products we offer in this growing sector, we offer quality and low price guaranteed products to small and medium-sized businesses. Our product groups include masking tapes, masking nylons, pre-paint preparation products, polishing products and sanders. For more detailed information about wholesale or retail sales, please send your request to info@uyaris.co.uk.

Industrial Products

We supply the auxiliary equipment, machines and consumables that every small, medium and large business and factory may need. We develop the needed products together with our solution partners in the domestic market or the foreign market and deliver them to you, our valued customers.

Everything About Food
